About Me

Hello! I am Alex Frankhouser. I started coding in 2022 and have fallen in love. What I love about coding is that there is so much to learn and improve upon. Also it is extremely satasfiying to see all the hardwork done in a project come together and make something amazing! I love putting my all into something and producing unique works of art. I am a black & white person who loves working with teams or by myself. No matter the situation I am faced with I will always stick it through! Previously in life I went to college to become a teacher and become a physical therapist assitant. I completed my Massachusetts Tests for Educator License (MTEL's). After completing my MTEL's, I started my schooling to become a Physcial Therapist Assistant. Once I graduated I realized that working in the healthcare field was not for me so I started my next venture by working full time at a wood yard. During this time I started from the bottom and worked my way up to become the production manager! Now I lead a team of four people and I am reposible for all the documentation and work that goes on.
